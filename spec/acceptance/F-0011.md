# F-0011: CO₂ Impact Info Panel — Acceptance Criteria

## Context
Players should be able to contextualise their CO₂ savings against real-world high-emission activities.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Opening and closing
- [ ] Press `I` to open the panel; press `I` again or click anywhere to close
- [ ] Panel does not open while the name-input dialog is active
- [ ] Clicking to close the panel does NOT plant a tree

### AC-2: Content
- [ ] Panel shows the player's current total CO₂ score prominently
- [ ] At least 10 real-world equivalences are displayed (flights, beef, car km, data centre hours, etc.)
- [ ] Each equivalence shows: emoji, calculated count, unit, and a short label
- [ ] Count is computed live from the current score at the moment the panel opens
- [ ] A sources note is shown at the bottom (IPCC, OurWorldInData, etc.)

### AC-3: Layout
- [ ] Panel is centred on screen at all window sizes
- [ ] Equivalences are laid out in a balanced two-column grid
- [ ] Full keyboard shortcut reference is shown in the panel footer

### AC-4: Accuracy
- [ ] CO₂ factors used are within accepted literature ranges (IPCC / Carbon Trust)
- [ ] Panel displays `< 0.1` for counts smaller than 0.1 instead of showing 0
